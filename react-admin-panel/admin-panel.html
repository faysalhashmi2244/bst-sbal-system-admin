Admin Panel Guide

Getting Started
  • Click "Connect Wallet" to connect your MetaMask wallet
  • Ensure you're connected to polygon mainnet (ChainID: 137)
  • Your wallet must be the contract owner to use administrative functions
Administrative Functions
  • Node Packages: Create, update, and manage node packages
  • Referral Settings: Configure seven-level and bulk referral rewards
  • Prosperity Fund: Manage fund settings and distribution
Troubleshooting
  • If transactions fail, check that you're on the correct network
  • Ensure your wallet has enough Base Sepolia ETH for gas fees
  • After network changes, you may need to reconnect your wallet
